Vegetarian Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg (or egg substitute)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 3 cups tomato basil pasta sauce
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the lasagna sheets as per packet instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic. Add zucchini, bell pepper, and spinach. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and a pinch of salt.
  5. In a baking dish, layer sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat the layers.
  6. Top with grated parmesan.
  7. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 35 minutes (cover for first 25 mins, uncover last 10 mins).
  8. Let it cool slightly, then serve warm.
